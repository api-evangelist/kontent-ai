Traditional monolithic CMSs show traffic stats in a sidebar. In the Headless CMS world, flexibility replaces visibility.
To address this, I created a Hybrid GA4 Dashboard for the Kontent.ai sidebar. By open-sourcing the implementation, I hope to save other developers from hitting the same technical "walls" I did.
